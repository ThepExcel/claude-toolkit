- **Market Penetration:** Market is growing, low market share, competitive pricing possible

- **Market Development:** Product has proven success, untapped markets exist, distribution capability
- **Diversification:** Core business is declining, significant resources available, risk tolerance high

### Analysis Steps

1. **List products/units** - All products or business units
2. **Calculate market share** - Your share vs largest competitor
3. **Assess market growth** - Industry growth rate (>10% = high)
4. **Plot on matrix** - Position each product
5. **Determine strategy** - Based on quadrant position

## Blue Ocean Strategy

**Use when:** Creating new market space, escaping competition, innovation strategy

### Red Ocean vs Blue Ocean

| Red Ocean | Blue Ocean |
|-----------|------------|
| Compete in existing market | Create uncontested market space |
| Beat the competition | Make competition irrelevant |
| Exploit existing demand | Create and capture new demand |
| Value-cost trade-off | Break value-cost trade-off |

### Four Actions Framework (ERRC)

| Action | Question |
|--------|----------|
| **Eliminate** | Which factors that the industry takes for granted should be eliminated? |
| **Reduce** | Which factors should be reduced well below industry standard? |
| **Raise** | Which factors should be raised well above industry standard? |
| **Create** | Which factors should be created that the industry has never offered? |

| Horizon | Focus | Time | Resource Allocation |
|---------|-------|------|---------------------|
| **H1: Core** | Extend current business | Now | ~70% of effort |
| **H2: Emerging** | Build new businesses | 2-3 years | ~20% of effort |
| **H3: Future** | Create viable options | 5+ years | ~10% of effort |

### Managing Across Horizons

| Horizon | Key Activities |
|---------|----------------|
| **H1** | Optimize operations, defend market share, maximize profitability |
| **H2** | Scale emerging opportunities, build capabilities, accelerate growth |
| **H3** | Research, experiment, pilot programs, strategic partnerships |

### Modern Consideration

With accelerating change, horizons compress. What was "5+ years" might now be "2-3 years." Monitor disruption signals and adjust accordingly.

- Brand strength
- Production capacity
- Profit margins
- Technological capability
